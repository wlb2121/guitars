The Story of SideShow Guitars



SideShow Guitars was started in 2015, when musician Wade Bounds decided to build his own instruments for his personal use.
After building his first Precision style bass and playing it live, Wade was approached to build instruments for other players.
Wade has built a few other instruments, mostly to give away to friends. He is now offering his instruments for sale.
